Holiday Break Announcement

We are pleased to announce that the Winter Holiday Break for the 2024 academic year will begin on December 20th, 2024, and classes will resume on January 6th, 2025. During this time, the campus offices will also be closed, but essential services such as the online library and student portals will remain available.

We encourage all students to use this break to relax, recharge, and spend time with loved ones. Any assignments due during this period should be submitted beforehand.

2024 Odd semester Timetable Released

We are excited to announce that the Fall 2024 class timetable is now available! You can view your course schedule, including lecture times, discussion sessions, and lab hours through the student portal.

Classes will begin on September 5th, 2024, and run through December 15th, 2024. Please make sure to review your timetable and familiarize yourself with the schedule before classes begin. If you encounter any scheduling conflicts or need assistance with course selection, please reach out to the academic office.
